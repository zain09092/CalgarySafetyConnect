Glyn Jones

Founder & Principal Engineer

Glyn Jones is the visionary founder of Calgary Safety Connect, launched in September 2025 as a registered non-profit society. With expertise as a Principal Engineer at EHS Partnerships Ltd. (part of Bravo Target Safety), Glyn recognized the need for a central hub where Calgary's safety professionals could collaborate, learn, and grow together.

Under his leadership, CSC has quickly become a thriving community, hosting workshops, luncheons, and social events that bring together safety leaders from all industries. His commitment to making safety education accessible and affordable has helped CSC expand its impact, even supporting the launch of Edmonton Safety Connect.
